# 🛒 E-Commerce Business Performance Analysis
### Olist Brazil Dataset | 115,000+ Orders | 2016-2018

## 📌 Project Overview
A complete end-to-end business analysis of Brazilian 
e-commerce platform Olist, covering revenue trends, 
customer segmentation, delivery performance, and 
actionable business recommendations.

## 🔍 Key Findings
- 📈 Revenue grew from near zero (2016) to 1.5M BRL/month peak
- 🏆 Bed/Bath/Table is #1 revenue category (1.67M BRL)
- 👥 52% customers are Loyal — strong retention base
- 🚚 Late deliveries score 2.5/5 vs 4.2/5 on-time (delivery = #1 satisfaction driver)
- 💳 73% customers prefer credit card payments

## 📊 Analyses Performed
- Revenue Trend Analysis (2016-2018)
- Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis
- Product Category Performance (Pareto Analysis)
- Delivery Performance vs Customer Satisfaction
- Geographic Revenue Distribution
- Late Delivery Impact Analysis
- Correlation Analysis

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. **Improve Delivery Speed** — Reduce late orders to boost ratings
2. **Re-engage At-Risk Customers** — 23K customers need campaigns
3. **Focus on Top Categories** — Double down on home & beauty
4. **Expand in RJ & MG** — SP saturated, these states show growth
5. **Replicate Black Friday** — Nov 2017 peak = proven strategy
